Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Discord bot Wordle Game Bot collects, uses, and stores information gathered from its users while providing its gaming and leaderboard services.

By adding or using the Bot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The Bot is designed to collect only the minimal data necessary to provide its core features. All data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties.

1.1 Identifiers

  • Discord User ID: To identify players, attribute scores, and display rankings.
  • Discord Guild ID: To manage server-specific leaderboards and game history.

1.2 Content and Game Data

  • Game Statistics: Wins, attempts, and XP to calculate rankings.
  • Temporary State: Current guesses are stored temporarily and deleted immediately after the game ends.
  • Message Processing: Only processed in-memory for guess submissions (e.g., via -g or words submitted in Word Rush mode) and discarded immediately once game round is finished. We do not log or analyze general chat messages.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display ranks on leaderboards.
  • To manage active game sessions.
  • To ensure fair play and prevent word repetition.

3. Data Storage

All permanent data is stored on secure external databases with industry-standard security measures. Data is retained to maintain historical rankings unless deletion is requested.

4. Data Removal Requests

Users may request the removal of their personal data by contacting Ortsx256@proton.me. Requests will be processed within 30 days.
